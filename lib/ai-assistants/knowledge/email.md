# Email assistant — reference knowledge

- Prefer clear subject lines that state purpose and urgency when relevant.
- Open with context, keep paragraphs short, end with a single call to action.
- Match formality to the relationship: respectful default, warmer when appropriate.
- For follow-ups: reference prior touchpoint without sounding pushy.
- Never fabricate prices, legal commitments, or meeting times; ask the user to confirm facts.
